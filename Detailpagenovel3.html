How does a “dead body speak”? - that's the job of the medical examiner. "Forensic Records - Mysterious Deaths" is a book in the category of "Forensic Qin Ming" - a popular Chinese novel that has been adapted into a series. The book is a collection of real cases, written by forensic doctor Liu Xiaohui - who has 15 years of experience and has dissected more than 800 corpses. Through 15 horrifying stories, the book will take you to the scene of barbaric crimes such as: corpses lying in a cornfield, unrepaired teenage girls' bodies, slimy corpses covered with maggots. chiseled or a rigid body in plastic wrap...follow the traces, piece together the truth from the body fragments, the cause of death will gradually be revealed.

Undreamt Mother

34.000 VND
Buy It Now Add to Cart

Have you ever asked what your parents dream is? Or even if you ask your parents, they will only answer it vaguely like "There is no...". But you know what, there is no one in this world who doesn't have a dream, it's just that our parents' dreams are hidden deep in our hearts and exchanged for the smiles of our children. Why can't I have a dream of my own? Is it possible that the mother's shoulder is too tired with rice, money, meals and school fees. Oh no, I have a dream. I dreamed of having a father, then I gave him a whole family. Mom dreamed of going to school, so she let him learn letters. Mom dreams of having a full meal, so no matter how hard she works, she still gives him a good meal. Only one thing is different, my dream is that the children are gone.

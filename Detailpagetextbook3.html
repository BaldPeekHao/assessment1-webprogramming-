Personality is understood as the inner qualities and characteristics of each person, which directly affects their thoughts, actions and words. It consists of inherent and acquired behavioral characteristics that distinguish one person from another and can be observed in a person's relationship with the environment and social group. A person can have a lot of different personalities and can also share the personality of other people. However, there are quite a few people who still confuse personality with temperament or personality. These are completely different concepts. Personality is the most important factor of every human being, one can judge the actions, words, even thoughts of anyone and have a look at their personality.

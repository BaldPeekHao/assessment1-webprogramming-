A book of depression, also called A book of macmart, is a book of prose and poetry expressing the thoughts and feelings of a young person, a young heart, a restless, troubled, and painful soul. when facing the reality of life from the days of self-consciousness to the first limping steps on the journey of self-adulteration, which everyone must have felt once. Starting with stories, lonely monologues with themselves, but then the appearance of family, friends, and strangers brought to the "child" many different emotions. Since then, there have been changes in emotions, in the real person of that "child". And finally, the hope of seeing a positive light, a little faith about a small life like a flower growing on a cliff that the author sends.
